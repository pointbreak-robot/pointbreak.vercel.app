### About Us

Point Break Robotics is an independent high school VEX V5 robotics team based in Fremont, California. 
We are a passionate group of student engineers, programmers, and designers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of competitive robotics. 
Competing in the VEX V5 Robotics Competition, we combine technical expertise with creative problem-solving to design, build, and program robots capable of taking on the world's best teams. 
Our mission is to foster a love for STEM, develop real-world engineering skills, and represent our community on the competitive stage.

Visit our website at: https://pointbreak.vercel.app
